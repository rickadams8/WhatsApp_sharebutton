What is sharebutton ?

install sharebutton to your website without hasle

Developed for the need to reach a big audience for my jobhuning campain

For my jobhuning campain, Rick Adams - Webdesign-Werk-Wandel-Tour it is a big plus to share my campainwebsite via WhatsApp.

Compatible with all modern browsers. Geeky time saver.

Tiny code. No downloads.

Connet with people cross socialmedia platform/browser
